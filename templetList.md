# Best Websites for Free HTML/CSS/JS Templates

Here is a curated list of the best websites where you can find free, copy-paste ready HTML, CSS, and JavaScript templates for your web development projects.

1.  **[Tooplate](https://www.tooplate.com/)**
    *   **Description:** Offers over 70 free, mobile-friendly, and responsive HTML website templates. They are built with Bootstrap and are very easy to edit without requiring registration.

2.  **[HTMLrev](https://htmlrev.com/)**
    *   **Description:** A massive collection of over 1900 free HTML/CSS templates. Categories include landing pages, portfolios, blogs, and dashboards. They also have templates for frameworks like Tailwind, React, and Vue.

3.  **[Freebiesbug](https://freebiesbug.com/)**
    *   **Description:** Curates high-quality free web design resources. Their collection includes over 150 free HTML website templates for landing pages, agencies, and portfolios, often using Bootstrap or Tailwind CSS.

4.  **[TemplateMo](https://templatemo.com/)**
    *   **Description:** Features over 600 free HTML CSS website templates. This includes a large selection (140+) of responsive Bootstrap themes available for personal and commercial use.

5.  **[Colorlib](https://colorlib.com/wp/templates/)**
    *   **Description:** One of the largest collections with over 1500 website templates. Most are based on Bootstrap 5 and feature clean, modern HTML & CSS.

6.  **[ThemeWagon](https://themewagon.com/)**
    *   **Description:** Provides a wide variety of free and premium HTML5/CSS3 templates. They are open-source and categorized by technology and design style.

7.  **[TeleportHQ](https://teleporthq.io/)**
    *   **Description:** Offers a suite of free HTML and CSS website templates. A key feature is the ability to access the source code directly for easy customization.

8.  **[Creative Tim](https://www.creative-tim.com/bootstrap-themes/free)**
    *   **Description:** Known for high-quality UI kits and dashboards. They offer free frontend and backend templates that often integrate with React, Node.js, Vue, and Angular.

9.  **[CodePen](https://codepen.io/)**
    *   **Description:** While not a template site in the traditional sense, it is an invaluable resource for finding specific components, animations, and creative code snippets to copy-paste into your projects.
